About the Project

What is the Project?

This project is an interactive psychological horror game inspired by the themes of The Sixth Sense. It explores fear, memory, grief, and the moment when someone finally understands a truth that has been hidden in front of them all along.

The player follows a man who wakes inside a strange hospital room and begins exploring unsettling spaces filled with clues, memories, and symbolic encounters. As the player progresses, the hospital changes depending on what they notice, collect, and understand.

The Idea Behind It

The idea behind this project is to create a horror experience that focuses more on emotion, atmosphere, and story than jump scares.

Like The Sixth Sense, the game is built around the idea that fear can turn into understanding once the truth is revealed. What seems frightening at first slowly becomes meaningful.

How to Play

The game is simple. At each stage, you'll choose between two options and continue your journey based on the decisions you make. Pay attention--every choice has consequences, and not everything is as it first appears. Which option is better? Maybe you'll find out... or maybe there was never a correct choice to begin with.

Good luck.
